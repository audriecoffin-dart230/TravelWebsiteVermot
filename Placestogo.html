Must Visit Places Located in Vermont!

  1. Chruch Street in Downtown Burlington
  2. Stowe Mountain Resort and Smugglers Notch
  3. Killington Peak
  4. Lake Champlain in Burlington

All of these must visits places in Vermont are affordable, or free!
When it is peak fall foliage, most of these places are going to be pretty packed with people.
So plan ahead, and accordingly to maximize your expierence!!
