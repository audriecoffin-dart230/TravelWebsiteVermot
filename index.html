Why You Should Visit Vermont This Fall

Things Vermont Has To Offer You This Fall Season

Beautiful Fall Foliage

  • Vermont has world class fall foliage. Vermonts mountains, valleys, and lakesides are covered in sugar maples and birch trees. The red, yellow, orange leafs are in full bloom from September to the middle of October. The activites during this time are endless, with perfect cool fall weather and beautfil scenary!

Apple Picking

  • There are hundreds of apple orchards and farms located all throughout Vermont, you can find apple picking in Vermont's beautfil fall scenary all throughout the state! The classic seasonal Vermont food are greatly on displace during the fall, the apple cider, the donuts, maple flavored everything, breweries and creameries are always open.

Hiking Opportunity

  • In Vermont you can find hiking nearly everywhere you go, Mount Mansfield, Camels Hump, Stowe, there are even trails that come from the long trail. Even if you are not into hiking, you can get that same expierence by heading to Stowe or Bolton and take a ride up their gondolas for a scenic ride up a mountain!

Historic Farms

  • Vermont farms are a crucial part of the society and agriculture that they offer, Shelburne farms pictured above have been a staple in Vermont for over 140 years. Shelburne farms tends to host many different festivals or concerts, all outdoors where you can enjoy all it has to offer whilst being outside! Most farms also have trails, lakefronts, and farm stands where you can get fresh local foods!
